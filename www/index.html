HOME ASSISTANCE
Source a tradesman to fix your emergency in double-quick time.
Help is at hand for those emergency household repairs (electrical, plumbing, locksmith or appliances) that could result in broader damage if they’re not attended to immediately. If essential services like electricity, hot water or sanitary use are compromised, call for urgent assistance.

Benefits:
  • Service providers are dispatched in the event of electrical and plumbing problems, locksmiths, glaziers or if an essential appliance needs to be repaired.
  • Assistance with three incidents per calendar year or per year from policy inception.
  • Service includes costs of call-out and first hour labour but excludes costs relating to parts.
  • Assistance can be provided for non-emergency repairs on a member-to-pay basis.
MEDICAL ASSISTANCE
An independent national network of at least 5,000 emergency medical personnel ready to respond to your call.
In the event of a medical emergency, our 24-hour emergency advice and assistance call centre will arrange critical medical assistance and transportation.

Benefits:
  • 24-hour life-saving assistance through the immediate dispatch of emergency medical services
  • Emergency transportation by air or road ambulance.
  • Safe transfer of children following an accident.
  • Repatriation/return of mortal remains in the event of a death as a result of a medical emergency.
  • Access to non-emergency medical transportation, at member’s own cost.
ROAD ASSISTANCE
Getting you back on the road.
Our 24-hour help line offers peace of mind by despatching the appropriate services in the event of a roadside emergency anywhere in South Africa. In certain instances non-roadside locations are accommodated too.

Benefits:
  • Mechanical and electrical breakdown
  • Jump-start service
  • Tyre change service
  • Running out of fuel
  • Keys locked in vehicle
LEGAL ASSISTANCE
Get telephonic legal advice from in-house attorneys.
Our 24-hour telephonic legal advice line is manned by qualified and experienced in-house attorneys who provide guidance and information on all legal matters, 24 hours a day, every day of the year. No matter is too big or too small and our service covers a wide range of legal concerns.

Benefits:
  • Unlimited general telephonic legal advice on everything from criminal offences, debt and labour matters to fines, contracts, divorce, maintenance issues and motor vehicle accidents.
  • Enjoy one free 30-minute consultation, one free letter and one telephone call from one of a national panel of attorneys.
  • Free use of any of the following standard legal documents: a Small Claims Court Kit, Child Maintenance Kit, Domestic Employment Agreement, Lease Agreement, Purchase Agreement, and a Last Will and a Testament.
  • Strenuous internal quality control measures and monitoring by an external legal advisory board.


TRAVEL ASSISTANCE
Complete peace of mind for you and your family’s health while travelling abroad.
Travel with the knowledge that the pioneers and market leaders of full service travel assistance have your best interests at heart.
The Europ Assistance SA medical team includes 15 professional nurses, 5 specialist travel-assistance nurses and 10 travel-assistance case managers, 25 paramedics,13 medical-case managers and 10 medical-support staff, a medical director (24-hour standby) and a divisional manager.

Benefits:

Pre-travel services and assistance abroad:
  • Telephone and online advice via Netglobers/EASA Hotspots, a community travel portal with current, relevant content.
  • Inoculation requirement information as specified by the World Health Organisation.
  • Referral to travel clinics and medical service providers.

Successful communication can only be achieved if you have a fully active data connection with your phone and your mobile network operator has a fully operation network coverage in the area. It is your responsibility to ensure that a working data channel exists, that the data transfer is acceptable and data transfer is possible. The service has been tested on the handset; we take no responsibility for the application not working on your handset for any reason whatsoever.
